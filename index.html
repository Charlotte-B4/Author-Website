Charlotte Burnett

Emerging Writer

Charlotte Burnett has been writing stories since she memorised all her Golden Words. She is currently studying a Bachelor of Arts (Creative Writing) RMIT and runs a primary school library. Her work aims to reflect the human condition in bizarre and imaginative ways.
